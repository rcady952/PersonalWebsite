Hello! Read on to learn why you should adopt a cat for yourself!

First of all, who doesn't love cats! They are cute, cuddly, and very independent creatures. They can be your loyal companion without too much responsibility, making them a great first pet. Cats are perfect for individuals who like a slower lifestyle.

Next, cats are very cleanly so you won't to worry about coming home to a smelly house. Of course you can wash them in a bath if needed but beware, it may not be the msot enjoyable for you or your cat.

Lastly, not only do you get to welcome a new freind into your home and give them a happy life, but cats can improve your life as well! Studies have been shown that cat owners expereince lower blood pressure, reduced stress, and even decreased chances of heart disease.
